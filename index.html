Adopt-a-Coral

Giving corals a voice in the Caribbean

Mission:

  • Provide a voice for corals in the Caribbean and show how they change in real time through crowd-science.
  • Democratize 3D imaging technology for coral reef ecology.
  • Build a community-based dataset to power the next generation of AI tools for coral research and management.

Caribbean reefs are the lifeline of coastal communities, providing food, coastal protection, and livelihoods. At the same time, they are dying at some of the fastest rates on the planet as stony reef-building corals turn from living structures into algae-covered rock.

Adopt-a-Coral is a crowd-science movement to capture corals over time, one coral at a time.
