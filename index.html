üåäRationale

Coral reefs are changing faster than most monitoring programs can keep up with. Divers see this every day, but their observations rarely become structured long-term data.

Adopt a Coral turns everyday dives into a collaborative time-series: short videos of the same colonies, converted into 3D models, tracked over years.

Goals
  • Build an open 3D dataset of Caribbean coral colonies.
  • Track growth, partial mortality, and bleaching at colony scale.
  • Provide training data for AI models to detect change automatically.

ü§øWhat you can do & how it works

  1. Choose a coral on your regular dive route.
  2. Capture a 20‚Äì30 second video circling the colony smoothly.
  3. Upload the video to the Adopt a Coral app / workflow.
  4. We generate a 3D model and add it to the public repository.
  5. Repeat every few months for the same colony (and new ones).
Practical tips for divers
  • Keep a constant distance and slow, stable movement.
  • Avoid heavy backscatter / night-torch hotspots if possible.
  • Note simple metadata: site, depth, date, colony nickname.
