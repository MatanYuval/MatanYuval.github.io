How to Adopt a Coral

  1. Choose a coral you like and can re-find (e.g. next to a mooring line or distinctive feature).
  2. Capture a 30–60 second video circling slowly around the coral.
  3. Make a 3D model from the video using a free app, such as:
    • Luma
    • Kiri Engine
  4. Fill in the participant sheet with basic metadata (site, depth, coral type if known, date, etc.).
  5. Upload your model to the open-source database [describe: Google Drive → Zenodo workflow].
  6. Return every few months to rescan the same coral and track its story through time.

You can find tips for better 3D underwater captures in the 3D Repository & Workflow section.
